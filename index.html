Gene

Your Personal Medicine Reminder

Gene is a Medicine Reminder assistant, and works on user's voice input. Gene remembers the time for your medications. He is on a mission to make his master take medicines on time. When it's time for medicines, Gene will notify you to take dose to take. Once the medicine is taken the user must turn-off the alaram. Gene takes voice inputs so you can assign add, delete timimings and names of medicines. The vision of this Project is to help remind people to take medicines on time as adviced by the doctor, as missing doses can worsen the disease in come cases and be fatal.
